PHASE 0
FOUNDATION ACADEMY
This phase is more important than all other phases.
Never assume learners know anything.

⸻
Sounds Academy
Teach:
What Hindi sounds are.
Tongue position.
Lip movement.
Voice vibration.
Breathing.
Common English mistakes.
Common Vietnamese mistakes.
Slow audio.
Native audio.
Speaking practice.

⸻
Vowel Academy
Teach ALL vowels.
अ, आ, इ, ई, उ, ऊ, ऋ, ए, ऐ, ओ, औ, अं, अः
Every vowel must contain:
Writing animation. Stroke order. Tracing. Audio. Images. Words. Mini stories. Speaking. Listening. Writing. Games. Quiz. Review. Celebration. 20+ interactions.

⸻
Consonant Academy
Teach all consonants. Grouped logically.
क वर्ग, च वर्ग, ट वर्ग, त वर्ग, प वर्ग, Semi-vowels, Sibilants, Special letters.
Every consonant:
Animation. Stroke order. Pronunciation. Tongue placement. Examples. Mini stories. Dialogues. Listening. Speaking. Tracing. Games. Quiz. Review.

⸻
Matra Academy
ा, ि, ी, ु, ू, े, ै, ो, ौ, ं, ः
Visual transformation.
Example: क ↓ का ↓ कि ↓ की ↓ कु ↓ कू

⸻
Reading Academy
Progress slowly: Letter. Word. Sentence. Paragraph. Story.
Never overwhelm.

⸻
Number Academy
0–100, Age, Money, Phone numbers, Time, Dates, Prices, Addresses.

⸻
Survival Sentences
My name is…, I am from…, Please., Thank you., Sorry., I don’t understand., Please repeat., How much?, Where is?, I need help.

⸻
Listening Academy
Very slow, Slow, Normal, Native. Male voices, Female voices, Children, Elderly voices.

⸻
Writing Academy
Tracing, Typing, Fill blanks, Sentence building.

⸻
Confidence Academy
Celebrate everything. “You can now read vowels.” “You can now read 50 words.” “You are ready for India.”

⸻

SEASON 1
ARRIVAL IN INDIA
The learner becomes the hero.
English Mode: John. Vietnamese Mode: Lan.

⸻

Rahul: Tea addict. Cricket fanatic. Makes terrible jokes. Forgetful.

⸻
BUILD MEMORIES
Not lessons. Memories. Birthdays. Cricket matches. Rainy days. Road trips. Movie nights. Arguments. Embarrassing moments. Family dinners. Festivals. Friendship. Love. Fear. Happiness. Pride. Belonging.

⸻
NEVER TEACH ISOLATED WORDS
Wrong: Apple = सेब
Correct: Rahul eats an apple. राहुल सेब खाता है।

⸻
NEVER TEACH GRAMMAR DIRECTLY
Hide grammar inside life. Hide grammar inside stories. Hide grammar inside conversations. Teach communication. Not rules.
